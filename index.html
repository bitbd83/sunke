HOW TO BUY
CREATE WALLET

Install TronLink from the official website, create a new wallet and secure the seed phrase, then transfer TRX to your TronLink address to buy a token on Tron Network.

CONNECT TO DEX

Connect to a DEX like Sun.io, Sunswap. Open the DEX website, click 'Connect Wallet', and select TronLink. Approve in TronLink to trade.

Swap TRX for Tokens

Select TRX in the 'from' field and your desired token in the 'to' field. Enter the amount, click 'Swap' or 'Exchange,' and confirm in TronLink.

manage YOUR tokens

Check your transaction status in TronLink under 'Activity' or on Tronscan. If the token isn't visible, click 'Assets,', 'Add Token', and enter the contract address.
